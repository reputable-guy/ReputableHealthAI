Sleep & Recovery
How It Works
Magnesium Glycinate helps regulate the body's sleep-wake cycles and has a calming effect on the brain due to its ability to bind to gamma-aminobutyric acid (GABA) receptors.
Key Findings
✅
A 2012 study found that magnesium can improve insomnia symptoms (Journal of Research in Medical Sciences)
✅
Magnesium supplementation has been shown to improve sleep efficiency (Journal of the American Geriatrics Society, 2019) *

4. Conclusion
Key Points
Conclusion
Magnesium Glycinate is a highly absorbable form of magnesium that has been linked to improved sleep, cognitive function, bone health, and metabolic health. However, more research is needed to understand its full range of benefits and effects.
Safety Considerations
Magnesium Glycinate is generally safe for most people, but excessive intake can cause gastrointestinal issues like diarrhea. Always consult a healthcare provider before starting any new supplement regimen.
